# NeuraCraft Code of Conduct  

## Our Pledge  

We, as members, contributors, and leaders of the NeuraCraft project, pledge to foster an open, welcoming, and harassment-free environment for everyone—regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.  

We uphold this pledge through our actions and words.  

---

## Expected Behavior  

The following behaviors are expected and encouraged of all community members:  
- **Respectful communication**, even when disagreeing  
- **Empathy** toward other participants  
- **Graceful acceptance** of constructive criticism  
- **Collaborative focus** on what’s best for the community  
- **Mindfulness of your surroundings/audience** in shared spaces  

---

## Unacceptable Behavior  

The following behaviors are unacceptable within any NeuraCraft-related space (including GitHub, forums, events, and private communications):  
- **Violence**, threats of violence, or intimidating conduct  
- **Derogatory language, jokes, or imagery** related to protected characteristics (see *Our Pledge*)  
- **Unwelcome sexual attention** or advances  
- **Trolling**, insulting, or dismissive commentary  
- **Public or private harassment**  
- **Sustained disruption** of discussions/events  
- **Retaliation** against individuals reporting unacceptable behavior  

---

## Reporting Guidelines  

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, **report it immediately** to the project maintainers at [conduct@neuracraft.io](mailto:conduct@neuracraft.io). All reports will be kept confidential.  

**Include in your report:**  
- Your contact information (optional but encouraged)  
- Names/identities of involved parties  
- Description of the incident (time, location, context)  
- Any supporting evidence (links, screenshots)  

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities  

Project maintainers are responsible for:  
1. **Clarifying** and enforcing this Code of Conduct  
2. **Taking appropriate action** for any reported behavior  
3. **Communicating consequences** transparently when needed  
4. **Maintaining confidentiality** in investigations  

---

## Enforcement Procedures  

**1. Warning (Minor Offenses)**  
- Private written notice to the violator  
- Clarification of the violation and expected corrective action  

**2. Temporary Ban (Repeat/Severe Offenses)**  
- Temporary exclusion from all project spaces (e.g., 30 days)  
- No public or private interaction with involved parties  

**3. Permanent Ban (Unrepentant/Severe Harassment)**  
- Indefinite exclusion from all community spaces  

---

## Scope  

This Code of Conduct applies to all NeuraCraft spaces:  
- GitHub repositories/discussions  
- Official chat channels/forums  
- In-person events (hackathons, meetups)  
- Public interactions when representing the project
